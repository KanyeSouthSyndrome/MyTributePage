🏋️ Exercise: HTML layout elements & basic CSS
In this exercise, you will build a tribute page of someone you admire in your life.


🛠 Requirements
On the top of the page, add the image and name of the person.
Below that give the layout for the rest of the details. You can use paragraphs, lists, links, images, etc.
Use CSS to round the image.
Add a suitable background color and font style to your webpage.
Most of the parts you can make using HTML but to give it a better look use a bit of CSS.
Post a screenshot of your finished exercise in the #post-your-exercises channel on Discord


🏆 Extra Credit:
Style the unordered list
Remove the bullet points and add a nice looking border to each row.
